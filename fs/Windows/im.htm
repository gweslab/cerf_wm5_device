    About Pocket MSN Messenger

    Pocket MSN Messenger delivers the features of MSN Messenger to your mobile device. Pocket MSN Messenger features include:

    • Seeing your Messenger contacts who are online or offline.
    • Sending and receiving instant messages.
    • Subscribing to status updates for selected contacts so you know when a contact comes online.
    • Blocking contacts from seeing your status or sending you messages.

    Getting Started with MSN Messenger

    To use MSN Messenger, you need a Microsoft .NET Passport. After obtaining a Passport, complete the following tasks:

    1. Set up a connection on your mobile device, such as a modem or wireless connection, that enables you to connect to the Internet.
    2. Set up an account in MSN Messenger.
    3. Sign in to MSN Messenger.

    		To obtain a Passport, go to http://www.passport.com.

    		To create a Hotmail account, go to http://www.hotmail.com.

    Set Up an Account

    Before you can use MSN Messenger, you must set up the instant messaging account to use. For MSN Messenger Service, you must have a Microsoft .NET Passport or Hotmail account.
    1. Create a connection for your device. To use MSN Messenger Service, create a connection to the Internet.
    2. In MSN Messenger, tap Menu > Options > Messenger tab.
    3. Select Enable MSN Messenger Service. Your sign-in name and password are established the first time you sign in to Pocket MSN.
    4. If necessary, select which account to sign in to first, under Sign in using this account first.

    		To obtain a Passport account, go to http://www.passport.com.

    		To create a Hotmail account, go to http://www.hotmail.com.

    Sign In and Out

    • To sign in, tap anywhere on the Messenger screen. Follow the directions on the screen.
    • To sign out, tap Menu > Sign out. Your status changes to Offline.

    		To sign in after connecting to a wireless network, tap Menu > Options > Messenger and follow the directions on the screen.

    		Signing in to an account may take several minutes, depending on your connection speed.

    Add and Delete Contacts

    • To add a contact, tap Menu > Add a Contact, and then follow the directions on the screen.
    • To delete a contact, tap and hold the contact's name, and then tap Delete Contact from the pop-up menu.

    Send a Message

    1. Tap the contact to whom you want to send a message.
    2. Enter your message in the text entry area at the bottom of the screen and tap Send. To quickly add common messages, tap Menu > My Text and select a message in the list.

    		To invite a contact to an ongoing chat, tap Menu > Invite and tap a contact.

    		To see who is already chatting, or to switch between chats, tap Menu > Chats.

    Subscribe to Status Updates for Selected Contacts

    When your device has an open data connection, status update notifications alert you when contacts come online or change their Messenger status.

    1. Tap and hold the contact's name.
    2. From the pop-up menu that is displayed, select the option to receive status update notifications.
